Tasty Chocolate Chip Cookies

These chocolate chip cookies are perfect if you like them large, chewy, and melty. The addition of bread flour creates a more cake-y yet chewy texture and helps the cookies retain their shape. The browning of the butter gives the cookies a deeper flavor without the grease. This recipe is from Tasty and its taste can be adjusted to fit your sweetness level.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup bread flour
  • 3/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 cup unsalted butter
  • 2 tablespoons water
  • 1 cup dark brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 2tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tsp espresso powder
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 large egg yolk
  • 1/2 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips
  • 1/4 cup dark chocolate

Steps

  1. In a bowl, sift together flour, baking soda, and salt.
  2. In a saucepan, make brown butter by boiling the butter until the milk solids form at the bottom and have become golden brown.
  3. Let the butter cool and add back liquid by adding the 2 tablespoons of water.
  4. In another bowl, cream together sugar, vanilla, esperesso powder, and the brown butter until fluffy.
  5. Add the egg and the egg yolk.
  6. Add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients.
  7. Fold the chocolate chips into the dough.
  8. (optional) Scoop portions of cookie dough and chill overnight for better flavor.
  9. Bake int he oven at 350 degrees Fahrenheit for 12-14 minutes.
